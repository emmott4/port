PORTFOLIO

REACH

Interactive application for the relatives to people with eating disorders.

Implementation

Group project with three-week design sprint

Course: Introduction to UX Production 2020

More: Reach was developed during a design sprint where the topic was social impact.

Unfortunately, there is a lot of people suffering from eating disorders today and throughout research we discovered that there is not a lot of support systems especially not for the relatives to someone with an eating disorder. To try to solve this we made a support system that is informative, accessible, and interactive. With the goal of helping relatives act accordingly and effectively in situations were most relatives feel they need help and in situations where the one with the eating disorder finds it that a lot of the time their relatives are acting “wrong”

BOINDER

Easy application for apartment change

Implementation: Group Project

Course: Tools for Prototype Production

We figured that there is a problem with the existing apartment switch applications because you can only search for it one way. “I want this” or “You want this”. A lot of people are moving from city to city today and it would help if you could search from both ways “I have this” and “want this” and have matched searches based on that. To solve this, we created the two-step form to fill in before you see your matches.
